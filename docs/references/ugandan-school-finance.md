# Ugandan School Finance Reference

## Context

SmartBursar is built for Ugandan educational institutions. The product should reflect how schools manage fees and expenses locally.

## Typical workflows

- students are registered by class and term
- fees are assigned per class or group
- parents may pay in installments or with discounts
- receipts are issued for each payment
- cashbooks are used to track daily collections and expenses

## Practical constraints

- schools may operate with limited internet or none at all
- computers are often older and may have limited RAM
- users may not be highly technical

## Implications for the product

- Keep forms simple and clear
- Preserve data locally
- Make reports easy to print and file
- Reduce dependence on external services
